Sadza

Sadza nemuriwo

This is the staple food in Zimbabwe. It is mostly eaten as supper in many Zimbabwean homes.


Ingredients

  • mealie meal
  • water

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Pour half a cup meali meal into a pot
  3. Mix this with cold water
  4. Now add the hot water into the pot and start stiring
  5. Stir until it is boiling
  6. Cover the pot
  7. Leave to boil for approximately 10 minutes
  8. Reduce the heat
  9. Add more mealie meal whilst stiring
  10. Thats it!
